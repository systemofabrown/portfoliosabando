About me.

fig. III Erick Sabando - Arch Designer

I'm Erick Sabando - an architectural design & visualization artist focused on the intersection of craft, systems thinking, and storytelling.

My work spans from conceptual ideation to tried and true building methods and experience. From speculative research and development to final design. I care about the details that separate mundane from anomalous.

Currently exploring how generative tools can extend, rather than replace, the designer's hand. Also, exploring immersive real-time rendering techniques that allow viewers to experience a developing design through third-person, first-person, and virtual reality - supplementing traditional delivery methods like not only static and fly-through renderings but interactive schematics and technical drawings. Putting stakeholders in the driver's seat as if they were playing an open-world video game.
